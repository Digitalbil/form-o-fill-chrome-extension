The rule editor

The rule editor is where you create the rule definitions for all you forms.

You can organize your rules into up to 6 tabs.
Clicking on a tab activates that tab and clicking again allows you to rename that tab.
Press the green floppy disc symbol to save your tab settings.

Remove a tab (and its rules!) by clicking on the minus icon on the right of the tab.
Add a new tab by clicking on the plus symbol on the last tab.
