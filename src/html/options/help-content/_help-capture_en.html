Capturing forms

To capture the state of a form you are currently seeing follow these simple steps:

  • Fill the form with all info you want to save. Don't submit that form!
  • Press the Form-O-Fill button () to the right of the location bar.
  • The extensions popup window will appear. Click the "Create one?" Link.
  • Form-O-Fill will find all <form> tags in the page and highlight them with an overlay Click the overlay of the form you want to extract.
  • A notification will appear telling you that the form extraction was successful.
    You can click that notification and it will take you to the options panel.
  • A message () on top of the rule editor allows you to append the extracted rule to the current tab's rule definitions.
    Click "Append extracted rule" and then save the rules.
  • Done.
